    Vintage: 2014 | New Zealand | Marlborough | Sauvignon Blanc

    The Marlborough region of New Zealand's South Island is world-famous for its premium Sauvignon Blancs, which have a distinctive aromatic, fruity, crisp character, with a fresh, vibrant aroma and flavours of gooseberries and lemons.

    About this bottle

    Thiis renowned style of Marlborough's Sauvignon comes partly from the climate there - plenty of sun by day but cool temperatures by night, so lots of fresh acidity is retained in the grapes. A long, slow ripening period in the autumn helps to develop the distinctive rich flavours found in this wine. This Marlborough Sauvignon Blanc was made especially for us by Brent Marris, one of the top winemakers in the region.
